# VetJobs — Design Brief (Figma + Launch Video)

## The product in one line
**VetJobs** — find real, scam-free jobs in Nigeria (and remote roles open to
Nigerians), and auto-apply with a tailored CV and cover letter.

## What it is
A job platform that does two things no Nigerian job board does well:
1. **Verifies every listing for fraud** — scores each job Verified / Caution /
   High-risk, so jobseekers never get scammed.
2. **Auto-applies for you** — set your role + CV once, and it applies to every
   matching verified job with an AI-written cover letter.

## Why it matters (use these for copy)
- In one recent recruitment, **1.9 million Nigerians applied for ~30,000 jobs.**
- Recruitment scams charge fake fees from ₦5,000 to ₦500,000+.
- Real jobs are scattered across WhatsApp, Telegram, and dozens of boards.
- VetJobs is the **trusted front door**: vetted jobs, zero scams, one-tap apply.

## Audience
Ambitious Nigerian graduates and early/mid-career professionals (tech, design,
data, marketing, support) applying widely — including remote roles abroad. Mobile-first.

## Brand identity
- **Personality:** trustworthy, modern, calm, optimistic. A protective ally, not a hustler.
- **Primary color (green):** `#15915a` → `#2cbd78` gradient. Green = "go / verified / safe".
- **Accent surfaces (soft pastels):** lavender `#e3dbfa`, mint `#d4f6ed`, peach `#ffe1cc`, pink `#fbe2f4`.
- **Neutrals:** ink `#15151f`, muted `#7c7c8c`, lines `#ececf3`, white cards.
- **Status:** verified green `#0f9d58`, caution amber `#c07b16`, risk red `#d64141`.
- **Type:** **Outfit** (geometric sans), weights 400–700. Headlines 600, not heavy. Generous spacing.
- **Look:** clean white surfaces, rounded cards (16–22px radius), soft shadows,
  pill buttons, skill-tag chips, a top nav on desktop + bottom nav on mobile.
- **Logo:** rounded green square with a white "V".

## Screens to design (Figma)
Design both **mobile** and **desktop (web app)** for the app screens.

**Marketing / landing page** (the hero of the deck):
- Dark or green hero: headline "Real jobs. Zero scams." + subhead + "Get started" CTA.
- A trust strip (verification badges), 3 feature blocks (Verified jobs · Auto-apply · Scam checker), social proof, footer.

**App screens:**
1. **Jobs feed** — green search hero banner; left filter panel (verified-only, job type); clean white job rows with company logo, title, meta (company · type · pay · location), short description, skill-tag chips, a **Verified/Caution/High-risk badge**, posted time, bookmark, green "Auto-apply" button.
2. **Verify (scam checker)** — paste a job message → animated safety score (0–100) + Verified/Caution/Scam result with plain-English reasons. Free, no account. (Two-column on desktop: form + "how we spot scams" card.)
3. **Auto-apply** — a big toggle ("On / Off"), live status (Applied · Matches · Skipped), and a list of applications, each expandable to show the filled form + AI cover letter.
4. **Dashboard (Stats)** — KPI tiles (total applied, this week, response rate, scams dodged), a response-rate ring, applications-over-time bars, status pipeline, by-role bars, and a filterable application list.
5. **Profile** — personal details, an "application answers" bank, and target roles each with an uploaded CV.
6. **Sign in / Create account** — clean, friendly, single card.

## Launch video brief (30–45s)
**Goal:** make a jobseeker feel "this protects me and saves me hours."
**Tone:** warm, hopeful, a little triumphant. Nigerian context.
**Music:** upbeat, modern, optimistic (Afrobeat-tinged).

**Storyboard:**
1. **Hook (0–5s):** quick, relatable frustration — a phone showing a fake job: "Pay ₦7,500 processing fee…" Red "⛔ Scam" stamp slaps on. VO: *"Tired of job scams?"*
2. **Turn (5–12s):** the VetJobs app opens, clean and green. Jobs scroll past, each getting a green **"✅ Verified"** badge. VO: *"VetJobs checks every job for fraud — so you don't get scammed."*
3. **Magic (12–28s):** user uploads one CV, flips on **Auto-apply**; a satisfying counter ticks up — "Applied to 12 verified jobs" — with AI cover letters writing themselves. VO: *"Set it once. We apply to every matching job for you — with a tailored cover letter."*
4. **Proof (28–38s):** the dashboard animates up — response rate, applications, "scams dodged." VO: *"Real jobs. Real applications. Zero scams."*
5. **CTA (38–45s):** logo + line: **"VetJobs — your job hunt, protected."** + "Start free at vetjobs.com". 

**Deliverables to request:** 9:16 (Reels/Status/TikTok) and 1:1 versions, plus a still
key-frame for posters. Captions on-screen (sound-off friendly).
